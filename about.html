Background & Expertise

With over 12 years of experience in legal education and practice, I have dedicated my career to making legal concepts accessible and understandable for students at all levels. I completed my B.A.L.L.B (Hons.) from National Law University and pursued my L.L.M specializing in Constitutional Law.

My teaching methodology focuses on breaking down complex legal principles into digestible concepts while maintaining academic rigor. I believe in combining theoretical knowledge with practical applications to help students understand not just what the law is, but why it exists and how it functions in real-world scenarios.

Throughout my career, I have taught thousands of students both in traditional classroom settings and through online platforms. The feedback and success stories from students inspire me to continue creating quality educational content.

Teaching Philosophy

"Legal education should not be intimidating. My goal is to create a learning environment where students feel confident exploring complex legal topics, asking questions, and developing critical thinking skills that will serve them throughout their legal careers."

Complete B.A.L.L.B Coverage

Our platform provides comprehensive study materials for all core subjects of the five-year integrated B.A.L.L.B program

Constitutional Law

Fundamental rights, directive principles, constitutional amendments, and landmark judgments that shaped Indian democracy

Criminal Law

IPC, CrPC, Evidence Act with case analysis, mens rea, actus reus, and criminal procedure essentials

Contract Law

Indian Contract Act with offer, acceptance, consideration, capacity, and breach of contract principles

Tort Law

Negligence, nuisance, defamation, strict liability, and vicarious liability with leading case laws

Family Law

Hindu, Muslim, Christian personal laws covering marriage, divorce, succession, and maintenance

Property Law

Transfer of Property Act, easements, mortgages, leases, and sale of immovable property

Administrative Law

Principles of natural justice, delegated legislation, judicial review, and administrative tribunals

Jurisprudence

Legal theories, schools of thought, natural law, positivism, and legal philosophy fundamentals

Legal Methods

Legal research, writing skills, moot court preparation, case analysis, and drafting techniques

International Law

Public international law, treaties, international organizations, and transnational legal frameworks
